Empowering young people’s entrepreneurship skills

What we do

The aim of the Promovent Project is to empower young people’s participation in entrepreneurship connecting the latter with the analogous challenge felt in participating European countries. The project provides young people with the knowledge, skills and motivation to plan and execute entrepreneurship activities and encourage entrepreneurial success. In the framework of the project several training events, workshops and youth exchanges are organized.
